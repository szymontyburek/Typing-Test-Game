Dark Souls is a series of action role-playing games created by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco

Entertainment. The series began with the release of Dark Souls in 2011 and has seen two sequels,

Dark Souls 2 and Dark Souls 3. The first title in the series is often cited as one of the greatest in

video game history, and the series as a whole has been both praised and criticized for its high level

of difficulty.
